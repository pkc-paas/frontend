About Us

Backgound

Pune and other cities have a range of plantation and greening initiatives that provide a tremendous base of knowledge, willing attitudes for greening and plantation. The ConnecTree Program will enhance existing urban greening initiatives and foster new ones.

Drawing from databases related to urban morphology, land records, weather and climate, soil types, vegetation and tree census, groundwater etc, as well as agent modelling and crowd sourced information, the ConnecTree program can enhance manifold the synergy and efficiency of logistics, diverse knowledge bases and citizens’ and corporate volunteer efforts.

The goal of this program is to increase the number of sapling plantations and enhance the chances of survival of planted saplings through a citizen engagement model.

This ‘ConnecTree’ Platform will evolve as a hub for sponsorship, information, engagement and management of plantations in urban areas. It is being piloted in Pune.

With data collection & a participatory tracking & monitoring mechanism, this initiative will develop a baseline for the survival rate of sapling plantation initiatives.

Participatory Activities

We appeal to corporates, individuals, social welfare and resident welfare associations to come forward and partner with us. Plantations in own premises as well as public premises can leverage the platform to track saplings, record phenological observations and exchange knowledge and learning.
